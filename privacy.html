Privacy Policy

AEDEXBOOKS — Business Management App by Aedex Anima LLC

Last updated: April 19, 2026 — updated Drive scope to drive.file

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Aedex Anima LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with AEDEXBOOKS, our web-based business management application available at aedexbooks.aedexanima.com.

By using AEDEXBOOKS, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

AEDEXBOOKS's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. What Information We Collect

Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following from Google's authentication service:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your display name (used to personalize the app interface)
  • A temporary OAuth access token, held in browser memory only and never transmitted to or stored on our servers

Business Data You Enter

AEDEXBOOKS stores the business data you create within the app — including clients, job records, invoices, estimates, expenses, and contractor information. This data is written exclusively to a Google Sheet in your own Google account. Aedex Anima LLC does not have access to, store copies of, or process this data on our servers.

Browser Storage

AEDEXBOOKS uses your browser's localStorage to cache your business data locally, allowing the app to load quickly and remain functional when offline. This data never leaves your device except when syncing to your own Google Sheet.

3. How We Use Google API Data

AEDEXBOOKS requests the following Google API permissions. Each scope is used for a specific, limited purpose and is not used for any other reason:

Google API ScopeWhy We Need It
Google Sheets
spreadsheets		 To read and write your business data (clients, jobs, invoices, estimates, expenses, contractors) to a Google Sheet stored in your own Google Drive. This is the app's sole data store — we do not maintain a separate database.
Google Drive
drive.file		 To create folders and upload files generated within the app: job photos (before/after/general), expense receipts, and PDF copies of invoices and estimates. Access is limited to only the files and folders the app itself creates — we cannot access any other files in your Drive.
Gmail
gmail.send		 To send invoices and estimates to your clients via email, on your behalf, from your own Gmail account. We request send-only access. We do not read, index, store, or access any of your existing email messages.
User Info
email profile		 To display your name and email address within the app interface, and to verify your identity against our access list.

Limited Use: Data obtained through Google APIs is used only to provide and improve AEDEXBOOKS. We do not use this data to serve advertisements, and we do not allow humans to read your Google user data except as required by law or with your explicit permission for security or support purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information or business data with any third party, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your own Google account: All business data is written to Google services (Sheets, Drive, Gmail) under your own Google account credentials.
  • Access control: Your email address is stored in Cloudflare's Key-Value infrastructure solely to determine whether you have been granted access to the app. No other personal or business data is stored there.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by applicable law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process.

5. Data Retention

Your email address is retained in our access control system for as long as your account remains active. If you request removal, we will delete it within 30 days.

Your business data lives entirely in your own Google Sheet and Google Drive. You control it completely — you can delete it at any time directly from your Google account. We do not hold copies of your business data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Revoke access: You can revoke AEDEXBOOKS's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • Delete your data: Delete your AEDEXBOOKS Google Sheet and any associated Drive folders to permanently remove all business data from Google's systems.
  • Request account removal: Email us at aedexanima@gmail.com to request removal of your email address from our access list.
  • Data portability: Your business data is stored in a standard Google Sheet. You can export it as CSV, Excel, or PDF at any time directly from Google Sheets — no special tools required.

7. Data Security

AEDEXBOOKS is served entirely over HTTPS. We do not operate any backend database that stores your business data — all data resides in Google's infrastructure under your own account, protected by Google's security systems and your Google account credentials (including any two-factor authentication you have enabled).

Access to the application is gated by an email-based allow-list stored in Cloudflare's infrastructure. OAuth access tokens are held in browser memory only and are never transmitted to or stored by Aedex Anima LLC's systems.

8. Children's Privacy

AEDEXBOOKS is intended solely for use by adults operating businesses. The application is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at aedexanima@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify active users via the app interface or by email to the address associated with your account. Your continued use of AEDEXBOOKS after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
